How it works

From first message to wedding day

Because many flowers need time to grow, we ask that couples reach out as early as possible — ideally 6–12 months before your date.

Fill out our wedding form with your date, vision, and the flowers you love. We'll respond within 48 hours.

02

03

Why timing matters for wedding flowers

Ideal lead time

6–12 mo.

This gives us time to source or start specific varieties from seed and plan planting around your date.

Minimum lead time

3 mo.

For seasonal flowers already in the ground, 3 months is the minimum — depending on your date and requests.

Deposit to hold date

25%

A deposit confirms your date and covers the cost of seeds, materials, and planning time dedicated to your wedding.
