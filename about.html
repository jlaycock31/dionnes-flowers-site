Our story

Rooted in the land, grown with love

Meet the grower

A lifelong love of flowers — and the soil they come from

What started as a backyard hobby has grown into something much more meaningful. For years, tending the garden was a personal joy — watching seeds push through cold spring soil, coaxing color from the earth season after season.

Now those same flowers find their way into the hands of neighbors, friends, and families celebrating life's most important moments. Every stem is cut fresh from our Augusta, Kentucky property — never shipped from a warehouse, never sitting in cold storage for weeks.

We believe flowers should feel as alive as the day they were grown — because they are.
